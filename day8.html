Day 8: Ashtami - Mahagauri Puja (Pink Theme)

Color: Pink
Goddess: Mahagauri, the pure and radiant form of Parvati.
Significance: Devotees seek her blessings for purity of mind and soul.
Activities: Puja, offering rose petals, and meditation.

On the Eighth Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Falguni Pathak will join us.

TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
