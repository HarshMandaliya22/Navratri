Day 6: Shashthi - Katyayani Puja (Orange Theme)

Color: Orange
Goddess: Katyayani, the warrior goddess.
Significance: Devotees seek her blessings for courage and victory over obstacles.
Activities: Puja, fasting, and singing hymns.

On the Sixth Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Falguni Pathak will join us.

DATE:20/10/2023
TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
PLACE: Jawahar Ground, Opp.Reliance Mall, Bhavnagar

Falguni Pathak is a renowned Indian singer and performer known for her immense popularity in the field of traditional and folk music, particularly Garba and Dandiya music, which are integral to the celebrations of Navratri, a major Hindu festival. She is often referred to as the "Dandiya Queen" for her contributions to these dance forms.
