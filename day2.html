Day 2: Dwitiya - Brahmacharini Puja (Royal Blue Theme)

Color: Royal Blue
Goddess: Brahmacharini, the ascetic form of Parvati.
Significance: Devotees seek the goddess's blessings for peace and prosperity.
Activities: Fasting, puja, and chanting of mantras.

On the Second Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Kinjal Dave will join us.

DATE:16/10/2023
TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
PLACE: Jawahar Ground, Opp.Reliance Mall, Bhavnagar

Kinjal Dave is a popular folk and traditional music artist from India, specifically from the state of Gujarat. She is known for her contributions to the Gujarati music industry, particularly in the realm of Garba and Ras Garba, traditional dance and music forms that are an integral part of Gujarati culture.
