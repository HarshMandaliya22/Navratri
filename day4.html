Day 4: Chaturthi - Kushmanda Puja (Green Theme)

Color: Green
Goddess: Kushmanda, the creator of the universe.
Significance: Devotees seek her blessings for strength and energy.
Activities: Puja, lighting lamps, and distributing prasad (sacred food).

On the Fourth Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Geetaben Rabari will join us.

DATE:18/10/2023
TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
PLACE: Jawahar Ground, Opp.Reliance Mall, Bhavnagar

Geetaben Rabari is a talented Gujarati folk singer from India who gained recognition and popularity for her soulful and captivating folk music performances. She is known for her contributions to Gujarati folk and traditional music, particularly in the Kutchi folk music genre.
