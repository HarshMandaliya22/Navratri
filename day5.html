Day 5: Panchami - Skandamata Puja (Grey Theme)

Color: Grey
Goddess: Skandamata, the mother of Lord Kartikeya (Skanda).
Significance: Devotees pray for the well-being of their children and family.
Activities: Puja, offering bananas, and bhajans (devotional songs).

On the Fifth Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Aishwarya Majmudar will join us.

DATE:19/10/2023
TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
PLACE: Jawahar Ground, Opp.Reliance Mall, Bhavnagar

Aishwarya Majmudar is a well-known Indian singer, primarily recognized for her contributions to the Gujarati and Bollywood music industries. She gained fame and acclaim at a young age for her melodious voice and versatile singing style.
