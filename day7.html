Day 7: Saptami - Kalaratri Puja (White Theme)

Color: White
Goddess: Kalaratri, the fierce form of Goddess Durga.
Significance: Devotees pray for protection from darkness and negativity.
Activities: Puja, meditation, and recitation of mantras.

On the Seventh Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Falguni Pathak will join us.
