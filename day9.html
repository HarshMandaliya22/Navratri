Day 9: Navami - Siddhidatri Puja (Sky Blue/Purple Theme)

Color: Sky Blue or Purple
Goddess: Siddhidatri, the bestower of supernatural powers.
Significance: Devotees pray for spiritual growth and divine blessings.
Activities: Puja, singing devotional songs, and concluding rituals.

On the Ninth Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Falguni Pathak will join us.

DATE:23/10/2023
TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
PLACE: Jawahar Ground, Opp.Reliance Mall, Bhavnagar

Atul Purohit is a renowned and highly respected figure in the world of traditional Gujarati music, particularly in the realm of Garba and Dandiya Raas. He is known for his pivotal role in popularizing and preserving these traditional dance and music forms that are associated with the vibrant Navratri celebrations in Gujarat and among the Gujarati diaspora worldwide.
