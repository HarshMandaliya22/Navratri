Day 1: Pratipada - Shailaputri Puja (Grey/Orange Theme)

Color: Grey or Orange
Goddess: Shailaputri, the daughter of the mountains (Shaila).
Significance: Devotees worship Goddess Shailaputri, seeking her blessings for strength and stability.
Activities: Kalash Sthapana, puja, and prayers.

On the First Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Kirtidan Gadhvi will join us.

DATE:15/10/2023
TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
PLACE: Jawahar Ground, Opp.Reliance Mall, Bhavnagar

Kirtidan Gadhvi is a popular folk and traditional music artist from India, specifically from the state of Gujarat. He is known for his contributions to Gujarati folk music, particularly the style of singing known as Dayro. Dayro is a traditional form of Gujarati devotional music that involves singing and storytelling, often accompanied by musical instruments like the harmonium and dhol
