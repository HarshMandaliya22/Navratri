Day 3: Tritiya - Chandraghanta Puja (Yellow Theme)

Color: Yellow
Goddess: Chandraghanta, who symbolizes courage and grace.
Significance: Devotees pray for courage and protection from negative influences.
Activities: Puja, offering sweets, and reciting hymns.

On the Third Day well known Gujarati folk singers named Jignesh Kaviraj and Kinjal Dave will join us.

DATE:17/10/2023
TIME:9.00 PM to 12:00 AM
PLACE: Jawahar Ground, Opp.Reliance Mall, Bhavnagar
